"Most businesses are overpaying vendors, running processes that don't need to exist, and managing suppliers they should have cut two years ago. Nobody told them because nobody wanted the awkward conversation."

CFOs and finance leaders who suspect their vendor spend is out of control but don't have the procurement depth to know for sure. Founders who've been signing contracts without anyone in their corner. Six weeks. Clear deliverable. You own it.

Every advisory engagement runs on the same structure. No open-ended retainers, no scope creep — a defined process with a hard deliverable at the end.
